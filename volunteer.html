Join the team that keeps communities safe.

No crazy time commitment. You help when you can. If you care about keeping people safe online, we want you here.

Day-to-Day Responsibilities

Volunteers are the backbone of RoSecure. Here's what you'd actually be doing.

Review Scam Reports

Verify reports submitted by the community, check evidence, and flag confirmed threats in our database.

Monitor for Threats

Keep an eye on Discord servers and Roblox communities for emerging scams, raids, and malicious actors.

Help Community Members

Answer questions, respond to support tickets, and guide people through reporting threats.

Contribute to Tools

Help build and improve our bots, website, safety databases, and documentation.

When you work here, you make a difference in every way possible.

What to Expect

No surprises. Here's exactly what volunteering with us looks like.

Flexible Hours

No minimum time commitment. Help when you can, step back when you need to. It's that simple.

Training Provided

We walk you through everything. No prior moderation or development experience required.

Real Impact

Your work directly protects real people from scams and malicious actors. This isn't busywork.

Great Community

You'll work alongside people who genuinely care about keeping others safe online.

Ready to join? Fill out our volunteer application and we'll be in touch.
