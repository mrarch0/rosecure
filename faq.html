Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RoSecure.

General

RoSecure is basically a safety tool for Roblox and Discord. We keep a big list of known scammers and bad actors, and we use that to protect servers from them. Think of it like a bouncer for your community.

Yep, 100% free. Everything we offer costs nothing. We're not gonna charge you to keep your server safe, that'd be weird.

Nope. We're completely independent. Discord and Roblox don't have anything to do with us, we just build tools for their platforms.

Bot & Features

Just click the bot invite link and pick the server you want it in. You'll need Manage Server permissions though.

You can check users, set up auto-verification, configure alerts, stuff like that. Type /help in your server to see everything.

Flags & Appeals

Hit up our chat support on the site or email hello@rosecure.org. Tell us your username and why you think the flag is wrong. We usually get back to people within a couple days.

Sometimes, yeah. If your appeal checks out or the original report was bogus, we'll remove it. But if it was something serious, the flag stays.

Moderation

We look at a bunch of different things. Keywords, images, their account history, links they share, who their friends are, what groups they're in, and more. It's a mix of automated checks and actual people reviewing stuff.

Green means you're good. Yellow means something's iffy and a real person needs to look at it. Red means we're pretty confident there's a problem.

For the really serious stuff, absolutely. Anything involving kids or threats of violence gets reported to NCMEC and law enforcement. We don't mess around with that.

Donations & Support

Head over to our Discord and check the store section. You can grab a one-time donation there. All of it goes toward keeping things running.

Nah, you don't get anything special for donating. It's just a way to help us out. Everyone gets the same service whether they donate or not.

Check out our Volunteer page. We're always looking for people who want to help out with modding, development, community stuff. Fill out the application and we'll get back to you.

Partners & Community

We work with groups like BloxKIT, BloxDefender, and The Goober Project. We share info on threats with each other so we can all do a better job protecting people.

DM us on Discord or shoot an email to hello@rosecure.org. We're always down to work with other groups that care about safety.

Yeah! Even though we're not partnered with them, we think Rotector and AEPE are doing solid work in the safety space. Worth checking out if you care about online safety.

Data & Privacy

Only what we need to keep people safe. That includes public user IDs, server IDs, and any reports or evidence submitted. We don't collect passwords, DMs, or anything private. Check our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.

Yes. Email us at hello@rosecure.org with your request. Note that if you have an active flag for serious offenses, we may need to retain certain records for safety purposes.

Absolutely not. We don't sell, trade, or share your data with anyone for commercial purposes. The only data sharing we do is threat intelligence with our safety partners, and that's strictly to protect people.

Yes. In line with Discord's Terms of Service and COPPA regulations, you must be at least 13 years old to use RoSecure's services directly. Server owners using our bot are responsible for ensuring their community complies with age requirements.

Safety Tips

Never share your password or login info. Don't click random links people send you. If something sounds too good to be true (free Robux, rare items), it's a scam. And make sure your account has 2-step verification enabled.

Block them immediately. Take screenshots of the conversation. Report them to the platform (Discord or Roblox) and submit a report to us through our Discord server. If it involves a minor and feels dangerous, tell a trusted adult and consider reporting to NCMEC at CyberTipline.org.

Join our Discord server and open a support ticket. Provide the user's ID, what they did, and any evidence you have (screenshots, links, etc). Our moderation team will review it.

Technical

The bot needs basic permissions like reading messages, sending messages, and managing roles. We don't ask for admin because we don't need it. Only the permissions required for safety features.

Parts of our code are available on GitHub. Our core detection systems aren't open source though, because bad actors could use that info to bypass our checks.

We're working on a public API for developers who want to integrate RoSecure checks into their own tools. Check our Documentation page for updates on that.

Getting Support

You've got three options: use the live chat widget on the bottom right of any page for quick help, email us at hello@rosecure.org for detailed inquiries, or open a support ticket in our Discord server for the fastest response.

Discord tickets usually get a response within a few hours. Email might take up to 48 hours depending on volume. For urgent safety concerns, Discord is always the fastest way to reach us.

Definitely! We love hearing from the community. Drop your ideas in our Discord server or email them to us. Some of our best features came from user suggestions.
